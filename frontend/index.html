What you get

Everything a payment protocol needs

LumenFlow is a complete on-chain payment stack — not just a token transfer. It covers the full payment lifecycle from acceptance to refund.

🏪

Merchant management

Register, verify, and manage merchant profiles on-chain. Merchants can update their details and receive payments immediately after registration.

🔐

Signature-verified payments

Every payment is backed by an ed25519 signature, preventing unauthorized charges and ensuring cryptographic proof of merchant authorization.

↩️

Full refund lifecycle

Initiate, approve, reject, and execute refunds — partial or full — with a 30-day window and on-chain state tracking from Pending to Completed.

✍️

Multi-signature payments

Require multiple approvals before a payment executes. Configurable thresholds and signer lists make LumenFlow suitable for treasury and escrow use cases.

📜

Payment history & analytics

Paginated, filterable, and sortable payment history for merchants and payers. Filter by date, amount, token, or status. Export-ready for accounting tools.

⚙️

Admin controls

Global stats, payment archiving, automated cleanup, configurable fee module, and contract pause/unpause — all protected by role-based authorization.

📦

Batch payments

Process up to 10 payments atomically in a single contract invocation. All succeed or all revert — no partial fills.

🔔

Real-time events

Rich on-chain events for every state change. Subscribe via Horizon SSE for instant webhook delivery to your backend — no polling required.

🧰

TypeScript SDK & CLI

A typed SDK for browser and Node.js plus a CLI tool for local dev and testnet workflows. Open-source and framework-agnostic.

How it works

From registration to settlement in four steps

LumenFlow is designed to be as simple as possible for merchants and payers, while giving developers full control over every step.

1

Register as a merchant

Call register_merchant with your business name, category, and contact info. Your profile is stored on-chain and immediately active.

2

Share a payment link

Generate a signed payment request with an order ID, amount, and token. Share the link or embed the payment button on your storefront.

3

Payer approves & funds arrive

The payer confirms the transaction in their Stellar wallet (Freighter or compatible). Funds settle directly to your merchant wallet — no custody, no delay.

4

Manage refunds & history

Approve or reject refund requests via the dashboard or contract API. Full payment history is always available for your records and accounting exports.

Who it's for

Built for real-world commerce

LumenFlow works for any business that wants to accept crypto payments without giving up control of their funds or their data.

🛍️ E-commerce

Accept stablecoin or XLM payments at checkout. Generate payment requests on the fly and confirm settlement in seconds — no payment processor fees.

🧾 SaaS & subscriptions

Issue payment requests for invoices and recurring charges. Partial refunds and prorated credits are supported natively without custom logic.

🌍 Cross-border payments

Send payments across borders without correspondent banks or FX markups. Stellar's near-instant finality means funds arrive in seconds, not days.

🏦 Treasury & escrow

Multi-signature payment flows require configurable approval thresholds before funds move. Ideal for corporate treasury, grants, and escrow arrangements.

🎮 Gaming & digital goods

Sell in-game items, passes, or digital downloads with verifiable on-chain payment proofs — no chargebacks, no fraud disputes.

🔧 Developers & integrators

Embed LumenFlow into any product using the TypeScript SDK, CLI, or direct Soroban contract calls. The open-source contract is auditable and forkable.
